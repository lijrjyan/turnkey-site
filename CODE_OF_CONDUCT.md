# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free community for everyone.
Contributors should be constructive, assume good intent, respect privacy, and
focus criticism on ideas and artifacts rather than people.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discriminatory language, threats,
doxxing, deliberate disruption, or publishing another person's private
information without permission. Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject
contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when
needed to protect the community.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through GitHub. Do
not open a public issue containing sensitive personal information. Maintainers
will review reports fairly, preserve confidentiality where practical, and
communicate the outcome to the reporter when possible.
